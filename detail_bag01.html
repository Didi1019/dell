FEATURES & Designs

Environment in Mind

Exterior fabric is made from approximately 7 recycled plastic bottles, which would have otherwise ended up in landfills. Packaging is made with 100% recycled content in the hang tag, hang loop and plastic bag. We use 420D ballistic body fabric to withstand daily use and back our product with a 3-year limited warranty.

Designated storage

A dedicated padded sleeve is an essential feature of our laptop storage solution, ensuring that your PC remains well-protected at all times. This sleeve is designed to accommodate a wide range of laptops, making it suitable for most devices ranging from 14 inches to 16 inches in size.

Stay organized

Our backpack features a convenient front organizer that's designed to keep your smaller items neatly stored and easily accessible. This organizer includes dedicated pen slots, slip pockets, and compartments, ensuring that items like pens, a mouse, or a power bank have their own designated spaces.For quick and easy access to essential items, we've included a zippered front pocket. This vertical pocket is perfect for storing items like phones or keys, allowing you to reach them without the need to dig through the main compartment.
