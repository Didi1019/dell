FEATURES & Designs

Advanced at every level

You asked for more cooling. Increased power. And better acoustics. We listened. Our improvements in Aurora R15 achieve 5% lower CPU temperatures while delivering 68% more power, enabling passionate gamers to play to their max potential.

Impressive thermal strategy

By revamping Aurora's thermal strategy, we improved acoustics and advanced our cooling, contributing to an incredibly smooth gaming experience. Airflow has increased up to 19% and CPU liquid-cooled systems now features a 240mm heat exchanger. Aurora R15 is also quieter than its predecessor, improving acoustics up to 66% during CPU intensive tasks and 32% in GPU intensive tasks.

REENGINEERED

Our internal architecture was engineered to support greater power and higher wattage. From the way we arranged cooling components to the repositioning of graphics and how UDIMM slots were laid out, everything was done to prioritize speed, performance and gameplay.13th Gen Intel® Core™ processors push limits, with Thread Director technology & Performance Hybrid architecture, delivering up to 32% more CPU performance gen over gen with Intel Core i9K processors on the R15.

MAINTAIN TURBO CLOCKS

Our advanced heat exchanger is twice the size of its predecessor, with a new 240mm radiator and dual 120mm fans. It's available with all liquid-cooled options, improving thermal resistance and pushing more power through next-gen Core CPUs. It also helps maintain turbo clocks under load and is designed for an overall performance boost.
