Get your game on

tailored exprience

You asked for more cooling. Increased power. And better acoustics. We listened. Our Aurora R15 desktop — fueled by AMD Ryzen™ 7000 Series processors with Zen 4 cores and 5nm technology — is up to 12% quieter while delivering 62% more CPU power and 47% more CPU performance than the Aurora R14 — enabling gamers, streamers and content creators to play, record and create to their max potential. All liquid-cooled configurations feature two fans pulling cool air in and three fans pushing hot air out, for improved airflow throughout.

thermal efficiency

By revamping Aurora's thermal strategy, we improved acoustics and advanced our cooling. Our latest 240mm heat exchanger is designed to increase thermal efficiency and overclocking potential, while new heatsinks help dissipate heat. Airflow has increased up to 19% with five 120mm fans and hexagonal side vents contributing to an overall performance boost.
