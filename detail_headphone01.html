FEATURES & Designs

Environment in Mind

Attend calls all day hassle-free with superb audio clarity and high-quality stereo sound. The on-demand active noise cancellation feature allows you to block out ambient noise and stay focused on your conversations. Switching off the active noise cancellation feature gives you the option to stay connected to your surroundings, even with your headset on.

smart call controls

Intuitive call features: Smart sensors automatically mute calls when you remove your headset and resume your call when you put it back on. Easy access to call features: Answer your calls, adjust the volume, redial, and mute/unmute on the go with convenient call controls on your headset.

all day comfort

Experience comfort and security with our headset, thanks to its leatherette earpads and adjustable headband. Charging is a breeze—simply place it on the charging stand or connect it to your PC via the USB cable. Seamlessly switch between various devices, from laptops to smartphones. When you're on the move, foldable earpads make packing up a snap, and the included pouch keeps your headset protected while you're on the go.

Intuitive features

  • Hearing protection

    This feature prevents audio spikes, safeguarding your hearing from sudden loud noises.

  • Real time voice prompts

    Enhanced audio alerts automatically announce your connection status, talk time and mute status.

  • Confirm your status at a glance

    The status indicator light on your USB dongle lets you know if you’re on mute or in a call.
