FEATURES & Designs

Endless performance

The world's most powerful 17-inch AMD Advantage™ gaming laptop is available in awide range of configurations, with up to 8-core/16-thread AMD Ryzen 6000 series processors, AMD Radeon graphics and AMD Smart Technologies. SmartTechnologies include SmartShift Max, Smart Access Graphics and Smart Access Memory.

LIFE-Like VISUALS

Dolby Vision™ uses studio and developer-defined metadata to bring you the most life-like gaming environments the world has ever seen. That means optimal color profiles and contrast that goes well beyond the capabilities of traditional high dynamic range (HDR).

Stun rivals, stay in awe

Never overlook a rival and increase your vertical field of view thanks to a 16:10 aspect ratio. Experience smooth gameplay with a wide range of 16-inch display panel options, ranging from QHD+ 165Hz through FHD+ 480Hz, all with response times rated at 3ms. All display options reap the rewards of the latest NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ graphics while also offering Dolby Vision™ and Dynamic Display Switching.

AMD RYZEN

Immerse yourself in the game with breathtaking, awe-inspiring visuals — all made possible with AMD RYZEN.

  • AMD SmartShift Max

    Available for the first time on an Alienware laptop, dynamic power shifting between a Radeon GPU and APU boosts performance for gaming, rendering and content creation.

  • AMD FreeSync™ Premium

    End choppy gameplay with adaptive sync technology for buttery-smooth performance at any framerate.

  • Realistic ray tracing in graphics

    Get an extra edge when your Ryzen processors are able to harness the full potential of the Radeon graphics card memory.

  • AMD Smart Access Graphics

    Improve frame rates, reduce latency and save battery by enabling discrete Radeon graphics or Ryzen graphics to handle rendering andpresentation while maintaining AMD FreeSync

Advanced audio

Enter new worlds through three-dimensional audio with Dolby Atmos®. Experience games as if you were in them, with sounds, voices and instruments placed in their own space. Plus, stay immersed with telliGo's AI-driven noise cancellation.

Immersive keyboard

The optional, low-profile mechanical keyboard delivers 1.8mm key-travel and a tactile, audible experienc unlike any other. Codeveloped with CherryMX, this keyboard also provides per-key AlienFX lighting and a smooth, wobble-free feeling when gaming. Plus, thanks to German engineered stainless-steel switches, gold-plated electrical contact enclosures and a 15 million keystroke lifecycle, you can type at your leisure with an assurance against failure.

LEGEND PERFECTED

Down to the last detail, Legend 3 design is more premium, tactile and futuristic than ever. At the rear, the iconic stadium shape now has soft edges, making our system easier to hold. A high-quality finish, resulting from bead-blast aluminum and a dark two-tone block, adds to the elevated look. Likewise, the overall design has shifted to focus on the total experience, with updated perimeter feet, a redesigned hinge and a new palm rest improving not only aesthetics, but also performance.

Tailored experiences

The latest version of the Alienware Command Center includes auto-tuned game profiles, intuitive overclocking options, easy-to-customize AlienFX settings and access to five unique power management modes tailored to different games. Modes include Performance, Balanced, Battery Saver, Quiet and Full Speed, which can be toggled using FN+F1.
