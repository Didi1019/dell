FEATURES & Designs

Compact Design

A 16:10 aspect ratio and 3-sided narrow border allows this laptop to have a larger 16-inch display screen built into a 15-inch laptop. This means gamers get 11% more screen space to play with on a 16-inch display in an easy to carry 15-inch laptop.

Optimized performance

The Alienware-inspired thermal design keeps the G16 performing optimally under pressure. Copper pipes, four strategically placed vents, and ultra-thin fan blades keep cool air moving through the system, ensuring the internal components are always performing at their best. Plus, with Windows 11 and up to 12th Gen Intel® i7 14-core processors, consistent high-end performance will be your new benchmark.

LIFE-LIKE VISUALS

Play over 100 high-quality PC games with your new Dell G PC and one month of Xbox Game Pass Ultimate for PC-including EA play. With new games added all the time, there’s always something new to play. Cloud Gaming with Xbox Game Pass Ultimate requires connected controller.

Stay cool in big moments

An Alienware-inspired, advanced thermal design with four heat pipes, and improved fans with ultra-thin blades increases the heat exchange area. Also, available with select graphics configurations, the Vapor Chamber and Element 31 thermal interface material combine to keep the laptop cool even during the most chaotic in-game segments.

Game Shift technology

Prepare for battle in seconds with the touch of a button, without having to exit your game. The F9 button is also the Game Shift macro key, which pushes the fan speeds to their max. The CPU detects this automatically and can shift into Dynamic Performance Mode, giving you the power you need to overcome those tougher in-game situations or complete CPU-intensive tasks.

LEGEND PERFECTED

Reign in the most graphically intensive games with up to 195W TDP at your fingertips. Warning: you may be awestruck by how real your games look.Impressive visual quality is possible with the Intel® Core™ Processors - coupled with a powerful NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ Laptop GPU. Minimize latency using Dynamic Display Switching technology through MUX when games get intense.Play locally and lag-free with up to 32GB of DDR5 memory and up to 1TB of internal storage.
