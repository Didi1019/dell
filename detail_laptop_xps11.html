FEATURES & Designs

Our most versatile XPS

Take on anything, anywhere with the XPS 13 2-in-1. From work to learning to life, use it as a laptop, sketch pad, or for video calls and streaming.

Faster downloads

You can get the fastest wireless speeds available, seven extra bandwidth channels, and an excellent internet connection with Wi-Fi 6E.

  • Capture and edit
    Capture high resolution images with the 4K, 11MP world-facing camera and edit on your XPS 13 2-in-1.
  • Connect with others
    Experience a clear picture quality on web chats and conference calls with a 1080p 5Mp webcam.
  • Snap and charge
    Magnetically snap the XPS Stylus on the top of the XPS 13 2-in-1 in either direction for simple, seamless recharging.
  • Vivid colors
    Enjoy a cinematic experience in the palm of your hands on the XPS 13 2-in-1. Enjoy vibrant colors with 100% sRGB color gamut.

Easier to use

A clean new design makes it much easier to search and find. Plus, new smart features anticipate your needs to help you get things done in a fast, fun, and easy kind of way. Search for websites and your files with one query, from the Start menu. Windows 11 can also suggest actions for your next step and people you are likely to share files with.Do more with Microsoft Teams, not just for work, but also to stay connected. With Teams on Windows 11, you can video call, chat, or text, right from your desktop.

Comfortable keyboard

A zero-lattice keyboard on the XPS Folio is fast, efficient, and comfortable with every keystroke. Expect an optimized user experience with larger keycaps, a spacious touchpad, and backlit keys that make working in low or no light situations easy.

Premium materials

The ultra-thin (0.29 in.) and ultra-light (1.60 lbs.) XPS 13 2-in-1 is made with chiseled CNC-s and low-carbon aluminum to reduce its carbon footprint and features packaging made from 100% recycled or renewable materials.

Unlock your productivity

The optional XPS Folio is a detachable keyboard that instantly transforms your XPS 13 2-in-1 into a laptop experience. The magnetic folio attaches at the base and supports three angle adjustments (100°, 112.5°, 125°) with magnets so you can adjust the position of your device.

The power of choice

Designed exclusively for the XPS 13 2-in-1, express your creativity with a natural writing and inking experience.
