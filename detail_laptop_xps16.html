Performance you need

Customize your laptop’s performance to suit your needs with the built-in Dell Performance application. Select from four modes - quiet, ultra-performance, cool or optimized. Default optimized mode enables the best balance of performance and thermals. When in ultra-performance mode, your system may run warmer, and fans may be louder. Choosing cool mode will limit temperature or decrease fan noise, which may impact system performance.

Comfortable keyboard

A dual sensor camera separates infrared from RGB, improving picture quality, even in low light. Plus, ExpressSign-in paired with Windows Hello streamlines your login experience, detecting your presence—or lack thereof—to quickly and securely sign you in or out of your laptop.

Modern Design

The minimalist and durable design, constructed with machined (CNC) aluminum, is inspired by nature and comes in a choice of two colors, Sky and Umber. Tonal, muted, natural color minimizes contrast on the interior, for a timeless look and feel.

More sustainable than ever

The XPS 13 uses low carbon aluminum in the chassis, thereby reducing its carbon footprint. It will also ship in all-new packaging made from 100% recycled or renewable content.
