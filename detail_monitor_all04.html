FEATURES & Designs

Vibrant hues, sharp contrast

IPS Black panel technology: Enjoy deeper blacks with the world's first 49-inch DQHD monitor with IPS Black panel technology that delivers exceptional contrast (2000:1) across a wide viewing angle. Equipped with 100% sRGB, 100% REC.709, 98% DCI-P3 and 98% Display P3 color coverage.

Improved Dell Display

Easy Arrange allows you to easily tile multiple applications across one or more screens with 49 pre-set window partitions and the ability to personalize up to five windows, giving you improved multitasking abilities.

Green thinking

This monitor uses 85% PCR (Post-consumer Recyclable) plastic in the design, 90% recycled aluminum and meets the latest environmental standards such as EnergyStar®, and TCO Certified Edge, and is EPEAT® Gold registered. Designed with the environment in mind, Dell commercial monitors come in 90% recyclable cardboard packaging. Save energy with PowerNap, a feature that dims or puts your monitor to sleep when it’s not in use.

Power of Two

Connect to two PC sources and Auto KVM (keyboard, video, mouse) will seamlessly switch controls over to the second connected PC. Use KVM feature to control both PCs with a single keyboard and mouse. The KVM wizard makes setup a breeze.
